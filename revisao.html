Unit 1 - Welcome: Introductions and greetings. Talking about personal information, such as name, nationality, and occupation. Expressing likes, dislikes, and preferences. Discussing daily routines and leisure activities.
Unit 2 - Numbers: Learning and practicing numbers from 1 to 100. Counting and identifying quantities. Talking about prices and money. Using numbers in various contexts, such as time, dates, and phone numbers.
Unit 3 - Work: Talking about different occupations and professions. Discussing job responsibilities and daily tasks. Describing work environments and workplace culture. Talking about future career plans and aspirations.

Unit 4 - Information: Gathering and sharing information. Asking and answering questions. Discussing personal information and preferences. Talking about news, events, and current affairs.
Unit 5 - Places: Vocabulary related to different places and locations. Describing and giving directions to places. Talking about travel and tourism. Discussing favorite places to visit and activities to do.
Unit 6 - Action: Vocabulary and expressions related to various actions and activities. Talking about daily routines and habits. Discussing hobbies, interests, and leisure activities. Describing past experiences and future plans.



Unit 7 - Meeting: Vocabulary and phrases for conducting meetings. Discussing meeting agendas and objectives. Practicing effective communication and collaboration during meetings. Negotiation skills and conflict resolution strategies.
Unit 8 - Reporting: Understanding the purpose and structure of business reports. Gathering and analyzing data for reporting. Presenting findings and recommendations in a clear and concise manner; Developing skills in written and oral reporting.
Unit 9 - Communication: Exploring different forms of communication in business contexts. Developing effective verbal and nonverbal communication skills. Practicing active listening and asking questions. Understanding cultural differences and adapting communication styles accordingly.

Unit 10 - Progress: Discussing progress and achievements in business. Reporting on project milestones and goals. Identifying areas for improvement and strategies for growth. Analyzing key performance indicators and metrics.
Unit 11 - Plans: Developing business plans and strategies. Setting goals and objectives for the future. Creating action plans and timelines. Monitoring and adjusting plans based on market conditions and business needs.
Unit 12 - Sales: Understanding the sales process and techniques. Developing persuasive communication skills for sales. Identifying customer needs and providing solutions. Building and maintaining customer relationships.
