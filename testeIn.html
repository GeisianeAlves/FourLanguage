Unit 1: Welcome

Introductions

  • Greet others: "Hello," "Good morning," "Good afternoon," "Good evening"
  • Share your name: "My name is [your name]."
  • Discuss your nationality: "I'm from [your country]."
  • Occupation: "I'm a [your occupation]."

Likes & Dislikes

  • Expressing preferences: "I like [something]," "I don't like [something]."
  • Talking about things you enjoy or don't enjoy: "My favorite hobby is [your hobby]." "I don't enjoy [something]."

Daily Routines

  • Describing your typical day: "I usually wake up at [time]," "I go to work/school from [time] to [time]," "I like to [hobby] in my free time."
  • Discussing work, hobbies, and leisure activities: "I work as a [your occupation]." "I enjoy [hobbies]." "I like to [leisure activities]."

Unit 2: Numbers

1 to 100

Learn to count and identify quantities using numbers from 1 to 100.

Time & Dates

Practice using numbers to discuss the time, dates, schedules and appointments.

Prices & Money

Discuss prices, handle financial transactions, and talk about budgets and savings.

Unit 3: Work

Workplace Culture

Workplace culture refers to the shared values, beliefs, and behaviors that characterize a particular organization or group of people within an organization. It can influence employee morale, productivity, and overall job satisfaction.

Types of Workplace Cultures

  • Hierarchical: Structured and top-down, with clear lines of authority.
  • Results-oriented: Emphasizes achieving goals and meeting targets.
